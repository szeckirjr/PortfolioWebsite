ENGR110 and ENGR120 - Design and Communication I & II

Both courses are divided into a design and a communication section.
For ENGR110, the communication section taught us how to do research and write a paper on a topic involving transportation methods. I wrote my research paper on the concept of the Hyperloop by Elon Musk and ended up getting 100% on it! You can read my essay here. The design section required us to make up a new viable method of improving transit in area. Our team presented Rentals of Electric Scooters and we got chosen to participate in a final presentation as one of the top 20 groups. Overall, our group ended up getting 4th place.
For ENGR120, the communication section was focused on technical documents (e.g. reports, manuals, memos, abstracts, etc). However, the design section was all about designing, building, and coding a robot using a VEX Robotics Kit. Out of my team, I was picked as the programmer and had to write code in RobotC to move the robot towards a beacon of infrared light, connect a cable to it, and back away for not getting untangled. After hours of programming and several days waking up at 5am for testing, our robot ended up performing very well and got chosen for the final competition as one of the best robots in the class.
Got a final grade of A for both of those courses.
